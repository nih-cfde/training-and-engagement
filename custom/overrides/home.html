About CFDE

The Common Fund Data Ecosystem (CFDE) Coordination Center (CC) is a NIH Common Fund effort to identify and solve issues that inhibit data access and reuse across Common Fund programs.

By connecting the data and making them more accessible, the CFDE aims to enable novel scientific research that was not possible before, including hypothesis generation, discovery, and validation.
